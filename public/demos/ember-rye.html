Our Story

Cooked with intention. Served with care.

Ember & Rye was born from a simple obsession — that the best meals come from the best ingredients, treated with respect and cooked over real fire. Every dish on our menu tells a story of the farm it came from and the hands that prepared it.

Our open kitchen puts the craft front and center. Watch our team work the wood-fired hearth, cure and smoke in-house, and build plates that balance boldness with precision.
